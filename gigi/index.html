Cards, the way Gigi taught you.

A cozy collection of classic card games for one player, against a table of computer opponents that play fair but don't go easy.

iOS / iPad version includes: Cribbage, Spades, Euchre, Hearts, and Texas Hold'em.

App Store Google Play Steam

What's inside

Every game plays by the real rules. Cribbage pegs properly and scores the full 29. Spades and Euchre are partner games here, like they should be, against bots that bid like they mean it. And Hearts still lets you shoot the moon.

Built for offline play

The whole thing works on an airplane. Your stats and achievements are saved on your device and nowhere else. See the privacy policy for details.

Controllers welcome

Full gamepad support on every screen. You can play the hub and every game end to end without touching the screen, which comes in handy on an iPad with a controller or on a handheld.
