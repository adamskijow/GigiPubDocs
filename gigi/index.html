Cards, the way Gigi taught you.

A cozy collection of classic card games for one player, against a table of computer opponents that play fair but don't go easy.

  • Setback
  • Cribbage
  • Spades
  • Euchre
  • Hearts
  • Texas Hold'em

All six games on every platform: iPhone, iPad, Android, and Steam.

Steam

What's inside

Each game follows its established rules. Setback scores High, Low, Jack, and Game. Cribbage pegs properly and supports the full 29-point hand. Spades and Euchre use partnerships, Hearts lets you shoot the moon, and Hold'em uses play chips only.

Built for offline play

Gameplay does not require an internet connection. Progress is saved locally; the Steam version may also synchronize its save through Steam Cloud. See the privacy policy for details.

Controllers welcome

Full gamepad support on every screen. You can play the hub and every game end to end without touching the screen, which comes in handy on an iPad with a controller or on a handheld.
