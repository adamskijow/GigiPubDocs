Steam

What's inside

  • Six games: Setback, Hearts, Cribbage, Texas Hold'em, Spades, and Euchre.
  • Computer opponents: Cribbage is head-to-head; the other games use four-player tables.
  • Four difficulty levels: Each game keeps its own setting, from Visitor through Gigi.
  • Game options: Setback and Spades include partnership and cut-throat modes. Hold'em is a no-limit Sit & Go using play chips.
  • Progress: Statistics and achievements are tracked for each game. Beating higher difficulty levels earns mastery and unlocks table felts and card backs.

Controllers welcome

Full gamepad support covers the game hub, settings, and all six games. Tested on Steam Deck.
