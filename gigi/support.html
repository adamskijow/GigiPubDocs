Frequently asked questions

Which games are included?
Cribbage, Spades, Euchre, Hearts, and Texas Hold'em. Every version has all five.
How do I report a bug or request a feature?
Email adamskijow@gmail.com with a short description of what happened, the game you were playing (Cribbage, Spades, etc.), and your device. Screenshots help a lot.
Does the game work offline?
Yes, completely. It needs no connection of any kind, so you can play on an airplane.
Does the game collect any personal data?
No. Your stats and achievements are saved on your device, and nothing is sent anywhere. See the privacy policy.
How do I reset my stats or achievements?
Uninstalling the app removes all locally-stored data. On iOS and Android there is no in-app "reset" button — uninstall and reinstall is the supported path.
My stats disappeared after updating. What happened?
That should never happen. If it does, email me with the version you updated from and to, and your device. That's a bug I want to fix.
Does the iPhone / iPad version sync with the Steam version?
No. The Steam version uses Steam Cloud for cross-device sync within Steam, but there is no sync between Steam and iOS or Android. Each platform keeps its own local save.
Are there in-app purchases or ads?
None of either. The game is a one-time purchase and that's the whole experience.
Does it support controllers?
Yes. Every screen is fully playable with a gamepad, including the menu and settings. On iOS, any controller that pairs with the system (MFi, PS5 DualSense, Xbox) will work.
Will there be more games added?
Yes, if there's demand. I'm focused on competitive card games, though, so don't expect solitaire-style additions.
Is there a kids mode / family mode?
It has no online features at all and collects nothing, so it's safe for kids in that sense. The card games themselves assume you know how to read; Cribbage and Euchre take a little explaining for younger players.

Still stuck?

Email adamskijow@gmail.com with as much detail as you can give. I'll get back to you.
