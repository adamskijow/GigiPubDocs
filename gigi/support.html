Frequently asked questions

Which games are included?
Setback, Hearts, Cribbage, Texas Hold'em, Spades, and Euchre. Every version has all six.
How do I report a bug or request a feature?
Email adamskijow@gmail.com with a short description of what happened, the game you were playing (Cribbage, Spades, etc.), and your device. Screenshots help a lot.
Does the game work offline?
Yes. Gameplay does not require a connection. Steam Cloud synchronization and reporting achievements to Steam are unavailable while Steam is offline, but the card games remain playable.
Does the game collect any personal data?
Joey Beans Games does not receive personal data from gameplay and the game has no advertising or analytics SDK. The Steam version uses Valve services for Cloud saves and achievements. See the privacy policy.
How do I reset my stats or achievements?
Open Settings from the main hub and select Reset progress twice to confirm. This clears local statistics, unlocks, and in-game achievements while keeping your settings. Achievements already earned on Steam remain earned there.
My stats disappeared after updating. What happened?
That should never happen. If it does, email me with the version you updated from and to, and your device. That's a bug I want to fix.
Does the iPhone / iPad version sync with the Steam version?
No. The Steam version uses Steam Cloud for cross-device sync within Steam, but there is no sync between Steam and iOS or Android. Each platform keeps its own local save.
Are there in-app purchases or ads?
None of either. The game is a one-time purchase and that's the whole experience.
Does it support controllers?
Yes. Every screen is playable with a gamepad, including the hub, game tables, and settings. On iOS and iPadOS, the game supports system-compatible MFi, DualSense, and Xbox controllers.
Does it have online multiplayer or chat?
No. Every game is single-player against computer opponents. There is no multiplayer, chat, advertising, or user-generated content.

Still stuck?

Email adamskijow@gmail.com with as much detail as you can give. I'll get back to you.
