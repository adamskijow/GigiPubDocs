A real card game, against the restless dead

This is the genuine nineteenth-century card game, two-handed All Fours (also called Setback or Auction Pitch): trump, tricks, and the four points, High, Low, Jack, and Game. Luck deals the cards; skill wins them. Each of the dead remembers one thing, and that memory bends the rules of the table. Learn what each has kept, and turn it against them.

Your candle is your life

The Wick is the only health you have, and the cold of the descent burns it lower with every layer. Make your bid and the rest you grant the dead feeds your flame; miss it and you are set back, and they take the difference. Between hands you draft charms, ghostly favors that reshape how you score or break a curse outright. Reach the bottom with the candle still lit, lay the town to rest, and learn the reason you cannot stop going down.

No ads, no accounts, no nonsense

One-time purchase, single-player, no advertising, and no data harvested. The game runs offline; when you are online, Steam Cloud carries your progress between the computers where you play it. See the privacy policy for the details.

Plays on a pad and on Steam Deck

Full controller support across every screen, from the title to bidding to drafting charms, with on-screen button prompts and in-game rebinding. No mouse required, and it plays on the Steam Deck.
