Privacy policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

All Fours in Ashes ("the game") is developed and published by Joey Beans Games LLC ("we," "us"). This policy explains what the game stores and how Steam features process data. Joey Beans Games does not operate an analytics, advertising, or player-data server.

Data received by Joey Beans Games

None from ordinary offline gameplay. The game has no analytics SDK, advertising SDK, developer-operated telemetry, or third-party advertising tracker. If you contact support, we receive the information you choose to include in your email.

Data stored on your device

The game may store the following data locally on your device:

  • Your campaign and town progress, unlocked Stakes and rites, and spirits laid to rest
  • Your daily and town score records
  • Your settings and key bindings
  • A snapshot used to resume an in-progress descent

This data lives in the game's private save storage. The game does not send it to a Joey Beans Games server. Depending on your operating system, uninstalling the game may leave local save files behind.

Steam Cloud, achievements, and playtime

All Fours in Ashes is distributed through Steam. When Steam Cloud is enabled for the game and your account, the main progress save may be synchronized through Steam Cloud. The in-progress descent snapshot and local settings are not included in that Cloud save. The game reports earned achievements to Steam, and Steam may record playtime. Valve processes this information under Valve's privacy policy. Joey Beans Games does not operate a separate server or receive the contents of your Steam Cloud save.

Steam leaderboards and score verification

When Steam leaderboard features are available, the game may submit your best Daily séance and town scores to Steam. A submission can include the score, date or town, game seed, Stake, selected rite, a version number, and a trace of gameplay decisions used to verify the score. The trace is stored through Steam Cloud user-generated-content services and attached to the leaderboard entry. Steam associates leaderboard entries with your Steam account.

To display rankings, the game retrieves leaderboard ranks, scores, Steam account identifiers, and Steam persona names from Steam. These features are used only to submit, verify, and display leaderboard results. They are governed by Valve's privacy policy.

Permissions

The game does not request access to your microphone, camera, contacts, location, or photos. The Steam version uses Steam account and profile identifiers as needed for the Steam features described above.

Children's privacy

The game is not directed to children under 13. Joey Beans Games does not knowingly receive personal data from children through the game.

Changes to this policy

If these practices change, we will update this policy and its "Last updated" date. Material changes affecting existing players will also be announced in the game's release notes.
