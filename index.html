App Store Google Play Steam

What's inside

Classic card games, each with the rules and feel you remember — proper Cribbage pegging with full 29-hand scoring, partner Spades and Euchre with bots that bid and play like real opponents, and classic Hearts with moon shots. The Steam and Android versions also include a tournament-style Texas Hold'em table; the iOS version does not.

Built for offline play

The whole game works on an airplane. There are no servers, no logins, and no analytics. Your stats and unlocked achievements live on your device, full stop. See the privacy policy for details.

Controllers welcome

Full gamepad support across every screen. The hub and every game can be played end-to-end without ever touching the screen — handy on iPad with a controller, or on a handheld.
